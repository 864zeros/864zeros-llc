How to Use

  1. Open the side panel Click the MigrationPilot icon in your toolbar. The panel opens on the right.
  2. Capture content (three ways)
    • Drag-select region — click the panel button, then drag a rectangle on any page
    • Save this page — full-text capture from the active tab
    • Right-click selection — highlight text, then right-click → 864zeros: Save selection
  3. Liberate to Markdown Click Liberate to Markdown in the panel (or right-click any page → 864zeros: Liberate to Markdown). Your captures download as .md files with full YAML frontmatter to ~/Downloads/migration-pilot/.
  4. Drop into your vault Move the .md files into your Obsidian / Capacities / Logseq vault folder. Done.

Tip: captures stack up locally (IndexedDB). Nothing leaves your device until you click Liberate.

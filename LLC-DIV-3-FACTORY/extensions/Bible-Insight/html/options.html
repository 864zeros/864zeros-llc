Privacy & Your API Key

Your Gemini API key is stored only on this device, in chrome.storage.local. It is sent only to Google's official Gemini endpoint at generativelanguage.googleapis.com when you trigger an AI analysis. 864zeros never sees your key, your prompts, or your responses.

Your captured content (highlights, screenshots, PDFs) lives in IndexedDB on this device. It does not leave your device unless you click an action that explicitly uses the Gemini AI features.

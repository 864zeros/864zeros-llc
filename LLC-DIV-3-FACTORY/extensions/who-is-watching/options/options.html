[OIA] Who Is Watching

See who collects your data — analytics vendors, identity resolution, and consent violations exposed.

How to Use

  1. Open the side panel — click the toolbar icon to see the live vendor map for the current page.
  2. Browse normally — the panel updates as analytics, ad-tech, and identity vendors fire on each page you visit.
  3. Inspect a vendor — click any entry to see the network requests, cookies, and consent state involved.
  4. When the Sovereign Link Vault unlocks, your full vendor-detection history exports to a folder you choose, locally — never through 864zeros servers (per RULE-007).

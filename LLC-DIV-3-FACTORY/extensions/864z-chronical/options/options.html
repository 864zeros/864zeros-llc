[OIA] Chronicle

Capture every AI conversation. Keep it forever.

Chronicle silently records your conversations with Gemini, Claude, ChatGPT, and Copilot — to a local database on your device. Your data never leaves you.

How to Use

  1. Open the side panel — Click the [OIA] Chronicle icon in your toolbar.
  2. Have an AI conversation — Visit gemini.google.com, claude.ai, chatgpt.com, or aistudio.google.com. Chronicle silently records the exchange.
  3. Browse, search, star — Open the side panel anytime to find what you said and what was said back.
  4. Liberate your vault — Tap the ⬇ Liberate Vault button in the panel header to export everything to your Downloads folder. Or use Data Management below for fine-grained control.

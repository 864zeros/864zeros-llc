TabVault by 864zeros

Stop losing your place and your privacy. TabVault is the high-performance archive that captures your entire workflow with lossless precision, remembering exactly where you scrolled so you can resume any session instantly. While other tools leave your data vulnerable to browser wipes, TabVault uses a permanent, local-first vault to ensure your research stays on your device—never shared, never tracked, and 100% ad-free. It's more than a memory saver that reclaims 95% of your RAM; it's a secure, "Deep Sleep" engine that keeps your focus sharp and your data yours.

How It Works

  • Two tabs at the bottom: Switch between Vault (saved tabs) and Sleep (open tabs)

Vault Tab

  • Vault Current Tab: Saves the current tab (with scroll position) and closes it
  • Vault All Tabs: Saves all tabs as a session group (e.g., "Feb 16, 3:42 PM")
  • Session Groups: Click header to collapse/expand. "Open All" restores tabs but keeps the group saved so you can return anytime
  • Restore: Click any tab to reopen it at its original scroll position
  • Search: Type to filter your vaulted tabs by title or URL

Sleep Tab

  • Sleep All Tabs: Puts all inactive tabs to sleep (frees ~95% RAM per tab)
  • Sleep icon (moon): Put individual tabs to sleep
  • Vault icon (lock): Vault a tab directly from the Sleep view
  • Sleeping vs Active: Sleeping tabs show a badge, active tabs have a green border

Auto Deep Sleep

  • Automatic: Tabs inactive for 20+ minutes are automatically put to sleep
  • Memory savings: Sleeping tabs use ~95% less RAM
  • Still visible: Sleeping tabs remain in your tab bar, just not loaded

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

TL;DR: Tune Out 2 Focus In collects no personal data. Everything stays on your device. We don't track you. We don't serve ads. Your privacy is protected by design.

1. Data We Collect

None. Tune Out 2 Focus In does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

2. Data Stored Locally

The Extension stores your preferences (selected sound type, volume level) locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your browser.

3. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not connect to any external servers or third-party services during normal operation. If you choose to make a purchase, that transaction is processed by our payment provider (ExtensionPay) according to their privacy policy.

4. Analytics & Tracking

We do not use any analytics, tracking pixels, or telemetry. We have no way of knowing how you use the Extension.

5. Advertising

Tune Out 2 Focus In contains no advertisements and never will.

6. Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we collect no data from anyone, this is not applicable.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at privacy@864zeros.com.

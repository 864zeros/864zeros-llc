### The Problem We Solved

In 2024, Kobo firmware updates broke native Instapaper sync. Instapaper's solution? Pay $30/year for Premium.

But here's the absurdity: **you're not paying for their data—you're paying to access YOUR OWN bookmarks.**

We call this the "SaaS Hostage Situation." Your data is held ransom behind a paywall.

ReadFlow breaks you out.

---

### How It Works

1. **Export your Instapaper library** (CSV file from instapaper.com/user)
2. **Drop it into ReadFlow** (Chrome extension)
3. **Generate a Kobo digest** (ePub format, one click)
4. **Transfer wirelessly** (QR code bridge or USB)

That's it. Your articles are now on your e-reader. Forever. No subscription.

## Why We Built This

The read-it-later market is broken.

- **Pocket** was acquired by Mozilla, then pivoted to recommendations
- **Instapaper** charges for basic sync features that were once free
- **Omnivore** shut down completely (RIP)

Users are left scrambling every time a service changes terms, raises prices, or disappears.

**ReadFlow is different:**
- No cloud dependency means no service shutdown risk
- Your data lives on your device, not our servers
- One-time purchase (Lifetime license) or free tier forever

We believe reading your saved articles shouldn't require an active subscription.

How to use ClipBoard
  1. Open the side panel — Click the ClipBoard icon in your toolbar, or right-click any page
  2. Capture content — Select text and click the + button, or use the capture menu for screenshots and full pages
  3. Organize with tags — Add tags manually or let AI suggest them for you
  4. Search everything — Use the search bar to find clips by content, title, URL, or tags
  5. Export anytime — Your data is yours. Export to JSON or sync to Google Drive (Pro)

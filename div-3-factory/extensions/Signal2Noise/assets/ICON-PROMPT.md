# Signal2Noise Icon Generation Prompt

## App Context

**Signal2Noise** is a Chrome extension for people with ADHD. It helps users capture daily thoughts/tasks ("signals") and mark the important ones, cutting through mental noise to focus on what matters.

**Tagline:** "Tune out the noise, focus in on your signal."

**Core metaphor:** Radio signal — clarity emerging from static/noise.

---

## Brand: OIA (by 864zeros)

**Voice:** Warm, calm, supportive, human. Never clinical, cold, or corporate.

**Design philosophy:**
- Soft, rounded, approachable
- Calming — reduces cognitive load
- Friendly without being childish
- ADHD-friendly: simple, clear, no visual noise

**What we ARE:** Warm, calming, supportive, soft, approachable
**What we are NOT:** Cold, corporate, aggressive, clinical, harsh, sterile

## Icon Requirements

**Sizes needed:** 16×16, 48×48, 128×128 px
**Format:** PNG with transparent background
**Shape:** Should work as a small toolbar icon (simple at 16px)

---

## Image Generation Prompt

```
Design a minimal, friendly app icon for "Signal2Noise" — an ADHD focus tool.

CONCEPT: A simple, abstract representation of "signal clarity" — could be:
- A single clear wave/pulse emerging from noise
- A tuning dial finding the right frequency
- A simplified radio wave or broadcast symbol
- A minimal "focus" or "target" symbol with soft edges

STYLE:
- Flat design, no gradients or 3D effects
- Rounded corners and soft edges (never sharp/aggressive)
- Minimal detail — must be recognizable at 16×16 pixels
- Warm and calming, not technical or cold
- Single focal element, no clutter

COLORS:
- Primary: Sage green (#8BA888)
- Optional accent: Soft coral (#E8A598)
- Background: Transparent
- NO pure black or pure white

MOOD: Calm, supportive, friendly, approachable. Like a gentle nudge, not a loud alarm. Think "cozy productivity" not "corporate efficiency."

DO NOT include:
- Text or letters
- Complex details that won't scale down
- Sharp angles or aggressive shapes
- Literal brain imagery
- Checkmarks or generic task symbols
- Pure black outlines
```

---

## Alternative Concepts to Try

1. **Radio Wave** — Single clean wave/pulse in sage, suggesting clarity
2. **Tuning Fork** — Simple U-shape, represents finding your frequency
3. **Bullseye/Target** — Soft concentric circles, focus without aggression
4. **Sound Bar** — 3-4 rounded bars of varying height (like audio levels)
5. **Lighthouse Beam** — Minimal beam cutting through (signal through noise)
